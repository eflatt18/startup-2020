What is Startup

StartUp is an annual event hosted by FLUX, the UX club at SCAD, in collaboration with SCADpro. Students compete in teams of 2-5 people in a week-long design sprint. The task is to come up with a product or service concept based on a theme that will be given on the first day of the event. Each team will have to deliver their concept in a form of a pitch and present to a board of judges. At the same time, students will receive hands-on mentorship from industry professionals. This past year, we had over 200 participants, in 47 teams and 26 different majors - our biggest one yet!
