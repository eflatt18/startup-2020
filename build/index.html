So what is StartUp

SCAD StartUp is an annual event
hosted by FLUX - the UX club in collaboration with SCADpro.

It’s a week-long design competition where students are challenged to come up with a product or service concept based on a theme, and are given a design toolkit of sources to utilize. Each team’s final deliverables are presented in the form of a slide deck which they present to a panel of judges that serve as mock investors.

This upcoming StartUp event is going to be on
February 7th - February 14th 2020.

Some questions
you probably have

What is SCAD StartUp

You know when you get unshakeable ideas that basically prove you’re genius? Well, this is a week-long design competition where you get to flesh them out with up to four other friends and pitch it to a group of mock-investors at the end. Similar to Shark Tank, just not on TV (yet).

Can I participate

To participate in this StartUp event, you must be a SCAD student. All undergraduate and graduate students are invited to join the party. Any year, any major.

How are teams set up

You’re more than welcome to plan your team ahead of time. Teams must be 2-5 people, and must represent a minimum of two different majors.

What if I don’t have a team

No worries, you don’t need to have a team before coming to the Kickoff event. You’ll be able to meet new people at StartUp Sessions following the Kickoff, and we’ll help you find fellow geniuses to work with. Just make sure to notify us on the registration form.

And if I’m crowned champion

First, second, and third place winners will win cash prizes (amounts will be announced soon). For all winners, you’ll receive extra merch, total bragging rights, and other cool stuff. And most importantly, you’ll walk out with something badass to put on your resume & portfolio.

Okay, and if I forget to RSVP

There’s still a chance! If people who RSVP’d don’t show up, a standby line will open at the Kickoff Event. It’s first-come first-served. So come early.

How much do I have to pay for this

It’s completely free.

Am I even qualified

Yes you are! Leave your resume at home. We know you’ve got skill.

What do I bring to the Kickoff Event and StartUp Sessions

1. Proof you go here. A student ID will do.
2. Laptop & phone
3. Chargers. Duh
4. Stuff that help you design.
5. Hunger to shake things up.
